Spicy Chicken Breast

Description:

A flavorful, high protein dish.

Ingredients:

  • chicken breast
  • paprika
  • salt
  • onion powder
  • dried thyme
  • black pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat lightly oiled pan to medium-high heat
  2. Mix together seasonings
  3. Rub seasonings all over chicken breast
  4. Cook chicken on preheated pan until juices run clear (6-8 minutes each side)
